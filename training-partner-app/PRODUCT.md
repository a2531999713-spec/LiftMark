# Product

## Register

product

## Users

LiftMark serves strength trainees who train alone or with a small local group on one Android device. Users are usually in the gym, checking today's plan, rotating sets with partners, and recording weights quickly without relying on a network connection.

## Product Purpose

练刻 LiftMark is a local-first strength training plan executor. It helps users run a structured plan, keep separate training identities for each member, record workout history in SQLite, and prepare clean boundaries for later accounts, cloud sync, groups, and membership.

## Brand Personality

Professional, direct, calm. The product should feel like a reliable training tool: clear hierarchy, strong data ownership, and no marketing-heavy account or membership surfaces in the middle of training workflows.

## Anti-references

Do not look like a generic subscription upsell page, a database debug console, an Excel sheet copy, or a single-user habit tracker. Avoid exposing trial mode, seed status, SQLite diagnostics, or destructive tools to normal users.

## Design Principles

1. Local training must keep working without the network.
2. Separate account identity from training member identity.
3. Put today's training and plan execution ahead of service marketing.
4. Make dangerous data actions explicit and reversible where possible.
5. Keep mobile layouts scannable in a gym context.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target Android portrait screens around 390 px wide. Text must stay readable on light and dark cards, controls need large tap targets, and unfinished features must respond with clear Chinese feedback instead of silent taps.
